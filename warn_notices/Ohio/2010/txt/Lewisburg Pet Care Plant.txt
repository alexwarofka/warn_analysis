The Pet Health and Nutrition Center in Lewisburg was not affected by this decision. Today, the Company has provided its employees with additional information regarding the timing of these events. In accordance with the policies set forth in the Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification Act ("WARN"), 29 U.S.C. д 2101 et seq., encouraging advance notice ofloss of jobs and employment transfers, the purpose of this letter is to notify you that: 1. 	It is anti~ipated that the Lewisburg Pet Care Plant, located at 6571 State Route 503 North, Lewisburg, OH 45338, will close between January 24, 2011 and February 6, 2011. Approximately 36 total employees will be impacted by this closure, with seven of those employees remaining with the Plant as part of a Site Clearance team until a period between June 17, 2011 and June 30, 2011. All affected employees will be given the opportunity to apply for positions at other P&G locations. If employees are unable to obtain a position, it is expected their employment will be terminated, and separation benefits will be available if necessary. 2. 	Approximately 160 employees at the Lewisburg Innovation Center located at 6571 State Route 503 North, Lewisburg, OH 45338, will be transferred to the Mason Business Center located at 8600 Mason-Montgomery Rd., Mason, Ohio 45040 and to the Winton Hill Business Center located at 6083 Center Hill Avenue, Cincinnati, OH 45224. The transfer of Pet Care R&D employees to the Mason and Winton Hill facilities is expected to occur between March 28, 2011 and April 8, 2011. 3. 	No bumping rights exist for affected employees at either site. # 3/11-18-10;02:59PM; Procter&.Gamble Rapid Response Section November 18, 2010 Page2 I 4.
