Act, at Kurz-Kasch, Inc., 511 Byers Road, Miamisburg, OH 45342. As of April 28, 2011, two of Kurz-Kasch's largest customers, Husco and Behr, suddenly and without prior notice, ceased communication and/or business activity with Kurz.Kasch. These two customers comprised 42% of the revenue for the Company's Miamisburg facility. Because of this sudden and unforeseen business circumstance, production schedules at Kurz-Kasch have been reduced to be aligned with our customer requirements. This has resulted in the layoff of 56 employees. These positions do not have bumping rights. The job classifications and numbers of employees laid off are attached.
