(Facility Closing) To the State of Ohio and Mayor Gorden: This is to notify you that DHL Express (USA), Inc. has decided to permanently close the following facility in the State of Ohio: PVZ station located at 23355 Mercantile Rd., Beachwood, OH. The effective date of the beginning of the closure is the two-week period commencing January 23, 2009, and, as of that date, employment of certain bargaining unit members by DHL will be permanently terminated. All positions and jobs at this facility will be eliminated. It is anticipated that approximately 130 employees will be affected by this action. Some of the employees are represented by the International Brotherhood of Teamsters, Local 407. Reduction in volume will be considered in determining the exact schedule of layoffs. This notice is being given to you pursuant to the Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification Act of 1988. Labor Relations Department 1210 5.
