Long Lake Rd., Suite 210, Troy, Michigan 48098 and 260 Gaige St, Jonesville, Michigan 49250. Consistent with these plans, employment separations in connection with the shutdown of operations are expected to commence on or about January 10, 2009. We anticipate that these plans, when finalized, would be permanent. At this time, the Company anticipates that groups of affected employees would be separated from employment effective January I 0, 2009, or during the 14 day period that begins on that date. A list of job titles, the number of affected employees currently holding affected positions, and the dates of separation that are currently known accompanies this letter as Attachment 1. The affected employees are not represented by any labor organization. Bumping arrangements are not available for affected employees. This announcement and the above timetable are based on the best information currently available. However, various factors may still affect the timing of any employment separations. You will be informed of any significant changes in these plans as additional information becomes available. To the extent that the above actions may constitute a plant closing or mass layoff for purposes of the Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification Act, this letter is intended to fulfill any requirements imposed under the Act. By providing this information, the Company does not concede that the Act applies or that notice is otherwise required.
