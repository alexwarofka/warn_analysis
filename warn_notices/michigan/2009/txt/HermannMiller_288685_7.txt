Plant operations will wind down over the next several months and will completely cease as of May 14, 2010. All positions and jobs at Integrated Metal Technology, Inc. will either be eliminated or integrated into other locations. The first date that employees will be terminated as a result of the closure of manufacturing operations at Integrated Metal Technology is September 8, 2009. In total, the number of employees that will be affected by the plant closure is 167. Some of these employees are represented by United Steel, Paper and Forestry, Rubber, Manufacturing, Energy, Allied Workers and Service Workers International Union, AFL-CIO-CFL, (USW), Local 2-410. The chief elected official of this Union is Randy Pelerine.
