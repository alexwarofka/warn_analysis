("Energizer") intends to begin undergoing a reduction in force at its Energizer Battery Company facility at 25225 Detroit Road, Westlake, OH 44145. As a result, approximately 120 employees will be laid off. Energizer anticipates that this layoff will be permanent. Bumping rights are not applicable and there is no union representation. Energizer expects that a majority of all layoffs will occur between January 31, 2013 and September 30, 2013. All affected employees will receive either 60 days notice, 60 days wages and benefits, or a combination of the two to ensure compliance with the WARN Act.
