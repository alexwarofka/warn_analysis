Attachment A enumerates the affected job titles and the number of affected team members in each classification. Any involuntary separations that do become necessary will begin on January 29, 2011. The affected employees are not represented by a union. This notice is being given to you pursuant to the Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification Act, 29 U.S.C. Section 2101, et. seq. A list of the affected employees by job title which includes the employees' termination dates will be available at the Waterford Target Store. For further information or coordination of assistance, please contact me at the above Target address or telephone me. Gary Mcfarren Regional Human Resources Business Partner RECEIVEDIN NOV1 0 2010 RAPIDRESPONSE ATTACHMENT A
