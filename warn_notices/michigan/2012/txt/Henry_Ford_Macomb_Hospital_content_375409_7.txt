Ten Mile Road, Warren, Michigan 48089, will close and cease operations as an inpatient community hospital. This action is expected to be permanent, and the entire facility is expected to close. All employees at the Warren Campus facility will either be reassigned or terminated. Henry Ford Macomb Hospital -Warren Campus presently has 1,030 full time, part time and contingent employees who will be impacted by this action. A list of affected job titles and the number of employees in each affected job classification is attached. Henry Ford Macomb Hospital -Warren Campus is a business unit of Henry Ford Macomb Hospital Corporation, which in turn is a wholly owned subsidiary of Henry Ford Health System. Although the plan is for the closure take place on March 31, 2012, the actual closure date may be earlier if necessary for business reasons. If this action occurs before March 31, 2012, the termination date for affected employees may be moved up as well. Likewise, the closure may be carried out in phases, in which event employees may be reassigned or terminated over time. All affected employees have been notified. Employees will not have any "bumping rights" as a result of this action. However, Henry Ford Macomb Hospital Corporation is presently attempting to place affected employees in other positions within Henry Ford Health System. There are 2 unions representing employees at the Henry Ford Macomb Hospital -Warren Campus, both of whom have been informed about the closure.
