Box 1618 Columbus, OH 43216-1618 Re: Notification of Layoffs at Southwest Ohio Regional Transit Authority Dear Sir or Madam: In accordance with ORC 4141.28(C), you are hereby notified that the Southwest Ohio Regional Transit Authority (SORTA/Metro) will be conducting a layoff of approximately fifty-five (55) bargaining unit employees due to a reduction in transit service. The layoffs will be effective December 27, 2009. Following is a list of the number and classification of jobs affected: 39 bus operator positions 
11 mechanic positions 
3 building maintenance positions 
1 storeroom position 
1 clerk/vault puller position 
Laid off employees will be placed on a recall list for a period of eighteen (18) months. All affected positions are represented by the Amalgamated Transit Union Local 627, 906 Main St., Suite 314, Cincinnati, OH 45202. Mr. Mark Bennett is President & Business Agent for Local 627. Although this layoff does not constitute a "mass layoff' under the federal WARN Act, SORTA is providing this notification as required by Ohio law. Please contact me if you need additional information or have any questions. Cc: Mr. Mark Bennett, A TU Local 627 c:.-,•METRO .(<,'"-·" www.go-metro.c om
