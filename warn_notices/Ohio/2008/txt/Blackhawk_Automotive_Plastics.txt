Front Street Columbus, OH 43216-1618 Re: Notice of Mason, OH Plant Closing Dear Sir or Madam: As you may know, Blackhawk Automotive Plastics, Inc. (the "Company") has commenced a reorganization case under Chapter 11 of the Bankruptcy Code. As part of its restructuring, the Company is pursuing a going concern sale of its business. By this letter, the Company is giving you notice that it is possible that a sale transaction will not include the sale of the Company's Mason, Ohio operations as a going concern. If the sale transaction does not include the sale of the Company's Mason, Ohio operations as a going concern, its plant at 419 U.S. Route 42, Mason, Ohio 45040 will be permanently closed. If the sale transaction does not include the sale of the Company's Mason, Ohio operations as a going concern, the first separation of employment will commence on April 25, 2008, and that it will be completed by April 30, 2008. The job titles of the positions that could be affected, together with the number of employees in each classification, are set forth on the enclosed list. Approximately 700 employees could be affected. As this will be a complete shutdown, no bumping rights will apply. None of the employees are represented by a union. If you need any further information with respect to the foregoing, you should contact Dan Thomas, Director of Human Resources, at (513) 229-4607. Sincerely, Blackhawk Automotive Plastics, Inc. Enclosures February 26, 2008
