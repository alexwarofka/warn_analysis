Re: 	Reduction in Force/Mass Layoff at FirstEnergy Generation Corp. Dear Ms. Maybriar Pursuant to the Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification (WARN) Act, please be advised that on or about September 1, 2012, FirstEnergy Generation Corp will reduce the number of employees via mass layoff at its Bay Shore plant located at 4701 Bay Shore Road, Oregon OH, 43616.
