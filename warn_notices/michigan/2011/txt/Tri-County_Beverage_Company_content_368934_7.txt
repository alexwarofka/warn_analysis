Re: 	Plant Closing at The Wolpin Compants Dearborn, Michigan Facility, Doing Business as Tri-County Beverage Company (Wayne) Dear Ms. Beckhorn: This letter is wlitten pursuant to the Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification ("WARN") Act, 29 U.S.C. § 2101 et seq., to notify you that The Wolpin Company ("Wolpin") currently expects to close its Dearborn, Michigan facility, doing business as Tri-County Beverage Company (Wayne), located at 14301 Prospect, Dearborn, MI 48126, commencing on January 2, 2012. It is currently anticipated that the closing of Tri-County Beverage Company (Wayne) facility in Dearborn will occur during the 14-day period between January 2, 2012 and January 15, 2012. Wolpin currently anticipates that this plant closing will be permanent and will result m permanent terminations of 66 non-union employees and 58 union-represented employees. We have enclosed a list of the job classifications that will be affected by the plant closing and the number of employees who will be affected in each of those job classifications. The affected employees do not have bumping rights to displace other employees from their jobs at other Wolpin Company locations due to the terminations associated with the plant closing. Union.represented employees may have bumping rights at the companies that are acquiring The Wolpin Company's business at Tri-County Beverage Company (Wayne). The affected union-represented employees are represented by the International Brotherhood of Teamsters, Local I 038.
