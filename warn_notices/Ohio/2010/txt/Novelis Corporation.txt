At this time, Novelis estimates that 191 full-time employees that work at or report into the Mayfield Heights location will be directly or indirectly affected by the office closure. Please note that of the total employees listed above, 62 accepted offers to transfer employment to the corporate headquarters in Atlanta, Georgia, 59 will not be impacted, and 70 employees will be permanently laid off . The job titles of positions that will be affected and the number of employees in each job category is attached. At this time, Novelis anticipates that the majority of the layoffs will occur on December 31, 2010. However, other layoffs may occur before or after that date, depending on the individual circumstances of particular employees. It is not expected that 50 or more employees will be laid off prior to December 31, 2010. None of the affected employees are represented by a union and there are no bumping rights. Novelis employees were notified of the December 31, 2010 closing of the Mayfield Heights office and their status with respect to that closing on February 11 and February 18, 2010, respectively . Novelis Colporation. ADITYA BIRLA NOVEL IS This notice is being provided to your office in compliance with the Federal Worker Adjustment Retraining and Notification Act ("WARN").
