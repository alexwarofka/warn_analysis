The number of employees affected is 108. The affected employees are expected to be terminated on the dates indicated on the enclosed list or within a fourteen (14) day period commencing on that date. There are no "bumping" or displacement rights for affected employees . All terminated employees will be paid their earned wages and applicable agreed-upon fringe benefits upon termination. Employees are not represented by a union . The information provided in this notice is based on the best information available to Cognis Corporation as of the date of this notice.
