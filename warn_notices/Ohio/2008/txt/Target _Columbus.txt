As a result, Target intends to permanently close its store at 5585 Chantry Drive, on March 3, 2009. During the next few months, we will be working with the approximately 133 affected non-exempt employees at the Columbus SE store to offer them employment opportunities in our new store at 2437 Taylor Park Drive, Reynoldsburg, OH 43068. Because all of our Columbus SE employees will be offered employment at the new store at 2437 Taylor Park Drive, Reynoldsburg, OH 43068, we do not anticipate that any will be involuntarily separated. Attachment A enumerates the affected job titles and the number of affected employees in each classification. Any involuntary separations that do become necessary will begin on March 3, 2009. The affected employees are not represented by a union. There will be no bumping rights. This notice is being given to you pursuant to the Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification Act, 29 U.S.C. Section 2101, et. seq. A list of the affected employees by job title which includes the employees' termination dates will be available at the Columbus SE store. For further information or coordination of assistance, please contact me at the above Target address or telephone me at (859)372-2033. Very truly yours, ~~ Lee Whitley Human Resources Business Partner Target Corporation G494 Human Resources, 1090 Hansel Avenue, Florence, KY 41042 ATTACHMENT A
