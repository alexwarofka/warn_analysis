It is not ce,rtain as to when the facility would restart production. Accordingly, pursuant to the Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification Act ("WARN") and its accompanying regulations, this notice is to advise you of a pending "mass layoff" as defined by WARN, at the Cleveland facility, 3060 Eggers Avenue, Cleveland, Ohio, 44105. All employees at the Cleveland facility will be affected, including those represented by United Steel Workers, Local Union 979 e'the Union") and those not represented by the Union ("non-represented employees"). A separate WARN Notice dated March 9, 2009, as well as a clarification dated March 10, 2009, were issued previously with regard to the employees represented by the Union. In addition, the non-represented employees now will suffer an employment loss during the 14-day period from May' 31, 2009 -June 13, 2009. However, the Company is in the process of offering all non-represented employees one of the following options, as decided by the Company based on business needs: 1) to perform critical casks at the Cleveland facility during the above situation; or 2) to perform a temporary reassignment elsewhere within ArcelorMittal USA Inc. Accompanying this letter as Attachment A is a list of affected job titles held by non-represented employees and the number of affected, non-represented employees within each job title.
