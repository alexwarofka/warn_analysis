This Act requires employers in certain circumstances to give official notice to the state dislocated worker unit for the state within which a pending plant closing or mass layoff will occur. Our understanding is that the Rapid Response Section of the Ohio Department of Job and Family Services is the appropriate body to which this notice should be directed.
