As a result, Varicon will shut down operations at its plants located at 133 White Horse Court, Greenville, South Carolina and at 23800 Corbin Drive, Bedford Heights, Ohio. The anticipated date of the shutdown is April 15, 2012. Until then, Varicon will wind-up operations and eventually clean and shutdown its facilities. Employees will be required to report to work during this period unless instructed otherwise. However, even if you are excused from work during this period, you will continue to receive your pay and benefits for sixty (60) days, through April 15, 2012. Varicon will also discontinue all of its employee benefit plans as of April 15, 2012, including its group health insurance, dental insurance, short and long term disability insurance, and its 401 k retirement plan. If you have any questions please contact me at 133 White Horse Ct., Greenville, SC 29605, at (864) 672-4865. Debra Gentry CFO -l ..:::J 0 ~:-~)-4 r,1 U)
