WARN Notice Dear Ms. Canfora: The economic downturn in the United States has dramatically impacted the market for steel and iron ore. As a result of this downturn in demand, Empire Iron Mining Partnership, which is owned by subsidiaries of Arcelor Mittaf and Cliffs Natural Resources, and supplies iron ore pellets to those companies for internal consumption and commercial sales, and Tilden Mining Company L.C. which is owned by subsidiaries of United States Steel and Cliffs Natural Resources, and supplies iron ore pellets to those companies for internal consumption and commercial sales, will be required to temporarily idle portions of their operations. At this time it is unclear how long the temporary idling may last, but it is likely to last beyond six months unless there would be an unanticipated upturn in demand due to a rapid economic recovery or some unforeseen event creating additional demand for iron ore from Empire Mine and/or Tilden Mine. Due to this indefinite idling of portions of Empire's and Tilden's operations. hourly employees at each location will be laid off on or after January 10, 2009. The names of affected employees who are currently known and their current job titles are shown on the attached schedule. Because of range wide bumping rights at Empire Mine and Tilden Mine. employees at each location are affected by reductions in force not only at their own mine but by reductions in force at the other mine. Reductions in force at each location will be made in compliance with the seniority provisions of the August 1, 2004 Basic Labor Agreement and Seniority and Posting Agreement. including any changes resulting from the recent September 1, 2008 Settlement Agreement. Benefits for employees laid off will be pursuant to the Basic Labor Agreement, Supplemental Unemployment Benefits Agreement. and Program of Insurance Benefits. The United Steelworkers represent hourly employee at Empire Iron Mining Partnership and Tilden Mining Company LC.
