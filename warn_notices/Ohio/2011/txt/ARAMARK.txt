While we have not determined that WARN is applicable to this closing, we are providing this notice in the event WARN is deemed to apply. The last day of ARAMARK's contract will be Wednesday, June 15, 2011, and all ARAMARK employees who are employed at the facility will have their employment terminated as of this date. No bumping rights are applicable to our employees as they will all be terminated due to this facility closing. The specific job titles of our employees affected by this facility closing, as well as the number of our employees in each job title, are listed on the attachment to this notice.
