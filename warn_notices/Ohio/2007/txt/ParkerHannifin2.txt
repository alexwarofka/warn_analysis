Dear Sir and Madam: The purpose of this letter is to advise you, pursuant to any obligations that may exist under the Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification Act ("WARN"), that the Hose Products Division of Parker Hannifin Corporation will permanently close its plant located at 33800 Lakeland Blvd., Eastlake, Ohio 44095 by approximately December 10, 2007. The first employment separations are expected to occur during the period of November 10 through November 23, 2007. The plant currently has about 181 Parker employees . It is anticipated that 181 employees at the Hose Products Division in Eastlake, Ohio will be affected by the closure. Some of the employees being affected are members of a union. There are no bumping rights to other facilities . We have notified the union and the employees and will provide WARN notices in accordance with any obligation that may exist. Any questions you have concerning this plant closing should be addressed to Jim Cartwright, Parker Hannifin Corporation, Corporate Communications Department, 6035 Parkland Boulevard, Cleveland, OH 44124-4141, Phone (216) 896-2752. Very truly yours, ~~CORPORATION ~
