Dear Ms. Canfora, In Faurecia's September 1, 2009 letter, we informed you that the information and notice in that letter were based on the best information then available concerning General Motors' plans for its Lake Orion manufacturing facility and the impact those plans would have on Faurecia's Auburn Hills facility. It is now apparent that the Faurecia Auburn Hills plant will officially close effective September 20, 2010. This letter is written pursuant to the Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification ("WARN") Act, 29 U.S.C. ¤ 2101 et seq., to notify you that Faurecia Automotive Seating, Inc. ("Faurecia") will close its Auburn Hills, Michigan manufacturing facility, located at 2800 Superior Court, Auburn Hills, Ml 48326, September 20, 2010. The closure of the Auburn Hills, Ml facility will result in the permanent release of g non-union employees and 159 union-represented employees a list of the affected classifications and the number of employees in those classifications is attached. The affected union-represented employees are represented by the International Union, United Automobile, Aerospace and Agricultural Implement Workers of America, UAW Local 5960.
