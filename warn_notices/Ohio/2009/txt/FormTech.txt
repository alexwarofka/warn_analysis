This will be a permanent closing of the entire plant and will result in a permanent layoff of all employees for the attached listed positions represented by Local 9478, USW. The plant closing will be accomplished through a phase-out of operations beginning on or about October 30, 2009 and is expected to be completed by the estimated date of December 31, 2009. Employees will be provided as much advance notice as possible regarding their last day of work during this phase-out period. This notice is provided in accordance with the requirements of the Federal WARN Act (Public Law 100-379).
