We expect that all migration of production from Oakley to our other facilities will be completed between September 20, 2010 and September 30, 2010. The total number of employees affected is sixty-eight (68) of which twenty-seven (27) are currently employed and forty-one (41) are laid off and eligible for recall. Affected employees who are in the union may have bumping rights. Affected employees who are not in the union do not have any bumping rights. A list of the affected positions is included. The union representing the employees at this facility is Sheet Metal Workers International Association Local Union # 24 and its chief elected official is Michael Moore.
