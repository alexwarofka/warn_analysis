City Hall Coshocton, OH 43812 Subject: Updated WARN Notice to State Dislocated Worker Unit and Local Government-Pretty Products, LLC Dear Governmental Officials: On October 10, 2007 you were provided with notice pursuant to the Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification ("WARN") Act of 1988, advising that Pretty Products, LLC planned to reduce and ultimately cease its production operations at the Coshocton facilities located at 437 Cambridge Road and 592 Clow Lane, Coshocton, OH 43812 on what the Company expected to be a permanent basis. Our first employment separations began on Saturday, December 15, 2007 and the facility at Clow Lane is now closed. It is now anticipated that all of the final layoffs at the Cambridge Road facility will commence on October 24, 2008 or within a period of 14 days thereafter. As of this point we expect the Cambridge Road facility to be completely closed by November 30, 2008. The separations of the union and non-union production employees will be permanent and will result in the elimination of all production operations at the remaining Coshocton facility. I have attached an updated list of positions that will be eliminated. Two collective bargaining organizations represent employees at Pretty Products, LLC; Local Union No. 50L, United Steel, Paper And Forestry, Rubber Manufacturing, Energy, Allied Industrial And Service Workers International Union, Steelworkers, AFL.CIO-CLC ("Local SOL") and Local Union No. 18-S, International Union of Operating Engineers ("Local 18-S") (collectively referred to as "Unions"). The chief elected officer of the Local SOL is Leo W. Gerard, whose address is Five Gateway Center, Pittsburgh, PA 15222, and the local chief elected officer is Gary Shearn, whose address is 500 N. Kirk Street, West Lafayette, OH 43845-1023. The chief elected officer of Local 18-S is Vincent J. Giblin, whose address is 1125 17th Street, NW, Washington, D.C. 20036, and the local chief elected officer is Kenneth M. Bumping rights exist for employees covered under the collective bargaining agreement between Pretty Products, LLC and Local SOL. No bumping rights exist for employees covered under the collective bargaining agreement between Pretty Products, LLC and Local 18-S or for unrepresented employees. If you need any further information, please contact me at (7 40) 295-5530. Sincerely yours, ~~.Sherri Bradford Human Resources Manager Enclosure PRETTY PRODUCTS, LLC -COSHOCTON FACIL TY Final Shutdown Schedule Classification As of 8/22108  # of positions to be eliminated on Oct. 24, 2008  CAMBRIDGE ROAD Shipping  Order Assemblers  2  Maintenance  Maintenance Janitor  4 1  Mat Dept.
