Box 1618 Columbus, OH 43216-1618 Re: Notification pursuant to the Worker Adjustment Retraining and Notification Act Dear Mr. Bimbrich: This is to notify you that Thomson Reuters ("Thomson") will institute a plant closing which will impact 132 of Thomson's full-time employees working at 6111 Oak Tree Blvd, Independence, Ohio 44131. The employment loss will be permanent and Thomson will cease its operations at its Cleveland location. Thomson expects that the first round of layoffs in connection with the plant closing will occur on or about April 9, 2010, and that subsequent rounds of layoffs will occur on or about June 18, August 13, September 24, October 8, December 17 and 31. This notice is being provided to your office in compliance with the Federal Worker Adjustment Retraining and Notification (WARN) Act. Additional information concemmg the job titles of positions to be affected and the number of affected employees in each job classification is maintained on site. If requested, this information will be provided to your office. Thomson will cooperate with the Ohio Department of Job and Family Services, and local government officials in any way that we can. There are, however, no provisions regarding transfer, bumping, or reassignment rights to affected employees in connection with their employment with Thomson. None of the affected employees are represented by a union. Notice of this closing is being simultaneously given to the Mayor of the City of Cleveland, the Director of the Ohio Department of Job and Family Services, and affected Thomson employees.
