This is to inform the Ohio Department of Job and Family Services per the Workers Adjustment and Retraining Act (WARN), that The Kroger Co., Columbus Division will permanently close store 595, located at 5827 Emporium Square, Columbus, Ohio on or around July 10, 2011. This store currently employs approximately 91 associates who will be given an opportunity to transfer to 
other Kroger locations in the area. Approximately 84 of these associates are covered under the collective 
bargaining agreement with UFCW Local 1059. We do not anticipate any associates refusing to transfer to 
other stores. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact me at 614-898-3555.
