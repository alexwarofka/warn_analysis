The number of employees affected is 171 (of which 131 are full-time or work at least 20 hours per week) . A list of the affected positions is enclosed. Affected employees do not have bumping rights. The Union representing the employees within the District is United Mine Workers of America, and its Chief District Representative is Phil Camden.
