(1988), please be advised that Graphic Packaging International, Inc. has decided to permanently close its facility located at 10600 Evandale Dr., Cincinnati, OH 45241. The expected date of the plant closure is August 31, 2011. The expected first separation will occur during the fourteen day period beginning on June 21, 2011. Separations will take place on the following schedule: June 21 -July 5, 2011, August 18-31, 2011, September 19-30, 2011 and December 19-31, 2011. Employees at the facility are unionized and the designated bargaining representative, United Steelworkers-USW, of these employees has been notified of this action. The name and address of the Chief Elected Officer of that Union is Ralph Brewster. Any non-union employees affected by this action will be notified individually. Nonunion employees do not have bumping rights. Union employees currently have bumping rights under the terms of the applicable collective bargaining agreement. Attached hereto on Attachment A are the job titles of positions affected and the number of affected employees in each job classification. All of the information required by 20 CFR д 639.7(e) is available on site for your review. For further information regarding this matter, you may contact Human Resources Director Jacinta Carter at 770-795-3819. CINCINNATI  CARTON  PLANT  Graphic  Packaging  International,  Inc.  10600  Evendale  Drive  е Cincinnati,  Ohio  45241  е (513)  733-2700  Attachment A EMPLOYEES WHO WILL BE SEPARATED DURING
