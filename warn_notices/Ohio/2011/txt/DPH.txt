August 2, 2011 Ohio Office Of Workforce Development 4020 East Fifth Street P.O. Box 1618 Columbus, Ohio 43216 RE: DPH Holdings Corp. Dear Sir/Madam: DPH Holdings Corp. ("DPH") is an entity formed to administer and liquidate retained, non-operating assets of the former Delphi Corporation, resolve and pay administrative expenses, and wind down all other affairs as expeditiously as possible. As a result of bankruptcy proceedings, DPH still has on its payroll a number of employees who are on lay-off or leave of absence, including 87 employees formerly employed at Delphi's Moraine, Ohio facility and 25 employees formerly employed at Delphi's Kettering, Ohio facility. As part of its effects bargaining with the IUE/CWA and the wind-down of its business affairs, DPH has proposed the permanent separation of its hourly workforce as of October 13, 2011. Accordingly, to the extent that a Notice under the federal W.A.R.N. Act is required, this is to notify you of the Company's proposed action. Please feel free to contact me at 248-813-2143 should you have any questions. Sincerely, John Brooks President
