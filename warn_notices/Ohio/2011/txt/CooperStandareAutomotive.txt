RE: Updated Notice Pursuant to Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification Act ("WARN"), 
Bowling Green Hose Plant. 
As was previously announced on February 4, 2011, Cooper Standard Automotive, Inc. has made a business consolidation decision, to better align the Company's North American Manufacturing capacity, that will result in the permanent closure of its Bowling Green Hose manufacturing facility located at 400 Van Camp Road, Bowling Green, Ohio. In accordance with the WARN Act, we are updating our prior notices to advise you that additional permanent layoffs (expected to be approximately 10-20 hourly positions) will commence during the 14-day window period beginning January 16, 2012. We continue to update and supplement our notices as a result of changing customer requirements, which result in the need to alter the timing of some of our business moves and layoff schedules. Our most current information now indicates that we will have some job losses (approximately 5-10 hourly positions) during the 14-day period beginning December 9, 2011 (for which a previous WARN notice was given on October 10, 2011 ); however, some of the originally expected job loss during that 14-day period may be pushed a number of weeks into the future due to changing customer requirements. Consequently, we are furnishing this updated notice. Because qualified bumping rights exist for hourly employees, it cannot be predicted at this time exactly when a particular hourly employee will experience job loss. No bumping rights exist for salaried employees. Details regarding classifications, names, and numbers of affected hourly employees were provided with our original notice but for reference, updated lists are attached again as Exhibit B & C. A Union does not represent the salaried employees. The United Steel Workers, Local 1152L represents our hourly employees. The chief elected officer/bargaining agent of the Local Union representing the hourly employees is as follows: Kevin West, President I Pat Gallagher, Rick Vermillion, International Union Representatives. You may contact me at the number listed below or Ms. Sharon Wenzl of our Corporate Communications Department at 248-596--6211 for further information. Sincerely, Patricia Scholl Human Resource Manager Cooper Standard Automotive 400 Van Camp Road Bowling Green, Ohio 43402 NA Fluid Systems Bowling Green Plant 419-353-2500
